What is Golf Manager?

Golf Manager is a web application that allows users to improve their golf game by analyzing courses and recommended strategies before playing.

How do I create a new account?

To add a new account, click on the "Log In" tab and click on the "Create an account link" button. Fill out the form and click "Submit".

How do I add a new course?

To add a new course, click on the "Home" tab and click on the "Recommend new Course" button near the bottom of the page. Fill out the form and click "Submit".

How do I add a new suggestion to a course?

To add a new recommendation, click on the course you have a recommendation for and on the "Add Recommendation" button. Fill out the form and click "Submit".

How do I edit my account?

To edit your account, click on the "Profile" tab and fill out any information you want to change then click the "Update Information" button.
