What is Golf Manager?

Golf Manager is a web application that allows users to manage their golf club's golfers, courses, and bookings.

How do I add a new golfer?

To add a new golfer, click on the "Golfers" tab and click on the "Add Golfer" button. Fill out the form and click "Submit".

How do I add a new course?

To add a new course, click on the "Courses" tab and click on the "Add Course" button. Fill out the form and click "Submit".

How do I add a new booking?

To add a new booking, click on the "Bookings" tab and click on the "Add Booking" button. Fill out the form and click "Submit".

How do I edit a golfer?

To edit a golfer, click on the "Golfers" tab and click on the "Edit Golfer" button. Fill out the form and click "Submit".
